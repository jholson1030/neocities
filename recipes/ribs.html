Tender oven baked ribs with a KC style barbecue sauce

This is a good recipe for the winter months. Let's say you really want dome dank-ass barbecue but don't want to freeze your ass off in front of a grill outside. I'm using a homemade barbecue sauce for this. I will make a seperate recipe for that then link it to this page.

Here's what you will need:


Spice Rub

  • 3/4 cup of brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup paprika
  • 2 TSP salt
  • 2 TSP granulated garlic
  • 2 TSP onion powder
  • 2 TSP chili powder
  • 1 TSP ground mustard
  • 1 TSP black pepper
  • 1 TSP celery seed
  • 1 TSP ground cumin
  • 1/2 TSP cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 TSP cinnamon

  • Mix all ingredients and set aside
