Nice spicy, savory and creamy dish that I make in bulk for meals throughout the week.

If you love spicy food then be sure to give this a try. Most people use maple syrup in their peanut noodles but I opted for brown sugar instead. Also used a few thai ingredients as well so you'll have to hit up your local asian market to find them. It will be totally worth it, I promise!

Heres what you'll need:

  • 1 pound dry egg noodles
  • 1 pound chicken breast
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onion
  • 3 tbsp minced onion
  • 3 tbsp chopped hot peppers (you can use whichever you want. I used finger peppers)
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp peanut oil
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp sriracha

  • 1 tbsp diced thai chili's
  • 3 tbsp fish sauce
