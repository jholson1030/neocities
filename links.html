Some links I think you should check out. They are for a wide array of subjects.

Site Description
YouTuube A great site made by Kitajima, who by all accounts, is an expert-level developer. Does a great job at parodying YouTube and bringing attention to how internet censorship on Youtube and other social media just gives rise to the dumbest content.
AI Dungeon Great site filled with different AI generated text adventures. No two are the same and is a great way to spend hours of your time!
Electronic Frontier Foundation The EFF has been around for a while fighting for your internet privacy. They have some cool stuff like Privacy Badger, a browser add-on that blocks ads and trackers. You can also donate to them and they have a shop. I'm not normally the kind of guy who throws away money to people online, but they are definitely worth it!
Chess.com Free site to learn and play chess. They have tons of lessons from beginner to grandmaster. Also can play against people around the world and bots. Great place, I love it!!
Prism-Break A curated site of programs for all devices for people who want to greatly reduce their digital footprint.
Cytube A site where you can stream movies, shows, videos and watch them with friends. Very cozy site, use it all the time!
Internet Archive The Internet Archive has been curating content on the internet both with the Wayback Machine and a giant library of content with books, music, software, movies, and tv recordings. It's definitely my go-to spot for nostalgic 90's TV blocks complete with commercials!
W3Schools An impressive site for web developers to learn everything they need to make great sites just like this one. Has documentation and tutorials.
Switched to Linux Tons of content about Linux. Great for beginners who want to make the switch.
Interpals International social media site to make friends in other countries. I use it to learn about all the different foods of the world :)
The Black Packet Site where you can watch tons of documentaries about a range of subjects.
Web3 is Going Great Site that documents crypto-scams and makes a great arguement against how Web3 is a giant grift.
9/11 In Real Time Takes you back to September 11th 2001 with news recordings, radio broadcasts, and other content.
PCjs Machines Online virtual machine simulator for old computers and operating systems.
AniWave Free anime streaming site. You can watch with english subtitles and dubs and everything is free!
Kung-Fu Chess Chess without turns. If you're into real time strategy games then you'll like it. Play a campaign mode, against an AI, or against a friend!
Vimm's Lair A site where you can find emulators for almost every game system and also had a whole vault of ROMs of your favorite games. Also has game manuals and a message board too!
